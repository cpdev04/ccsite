Our Mission , Vision & Opportunity

Our Mission

At Child Central Clinics we bring in all Pediatric services for your child under one roof.
We believe in providing holistic pediatric care which will help in shaping up a healthy future for your child.

Vision

Our team of experienced doctors believe in providing exceptional health service for your child.
Our comprehensive Pediatric health checkup programs aim at prevention and early detection of pediatric issues. We listen..We care..We are always there for your child.

Opportunity

At Child Central Clinic, we are always looking for passionate and dedicated professionals who are committed to making a difference in pediatric healthcare.
If you are driven by compassion, innovation, and a desire to help children grow healthier and stronger, we invite you to join our team.

Dr. Jagdish Chinnappa

Chief Pediatric Consultant

With over 46 years of experience, Dr. Jagdish Chinnappa is a highly respected pediatrician known for his expertise in diagnosing and treating complex pediatric conditions. He completed his MD in Pediatrics from Grant Medical College, Mumbai, and is an active member of the Indian Academy of Pediatrics. Dr. Chinnappa is available for consultations at Child Central Clinic and Manipal Hospital, Old Airport Road.

Dr. Aruna Jagdish

Senior Pediatrician

Dr. Aruna Jagdish brings 44 years of experience in pediatrics and has a compassionate approach to child care. She holds an MBBS and a Diploma in Child Health (DCH) from the University of Bombay. Known for her empathetic patient care, she practices at Child Central Clinic, Koramangala.

Dr. Abhinav Malhotra

Senior Pediatric Consultant

Dr. Abhinav Malhotra, with over 15 years of experience, specializes in newborn care, vaccinations, and allergy and asthma management. He holds an MD in Pediatrics from Grant Medical College and a fellowship from CMC Vellore. He consults at Child Central Clinic, Narayana Hrudayalaya HSR, and Happy Kids Child Care Clinic, HSR Layout.

Dr. Chandan Gaddehosur

Senior Pedodontist

Dr. Chandan has 17 years of experience in pediatric dentistry. He specializes in preventive dentistry, dental care for special needs children, and dentistry under general anesthesia. He completed his BDS and MDS from VS Dental College & Hospital, Bangalore, and practices at Child Central Clinic and KIMS Hospital.

Dr. Shradha Malhotra

Head of Labs and Diagnostics | Senior Consultant Radiologist

Dr. Shradha is a double gold medalist with over 15 years of experience. She has worked at leading diagnostic centers like Medall Clumax and Motherhood Hospitals. As the head of labs at Child Central Clinic, she ensures efficient diagnostics with quick turnaround times and accurate reports

Anagha Pandit

Clinical Psychologist, CBT Practitioner, NLP Master Practitioner

Anagha Pandit is a clinical psychologist with 14+ years of experience providing compassionate and evidence-based mental health care.

She holds a Masters degree in Clinical Psychology from S.N.D.T. Women's University,(Pune Campus). Her expertise in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) equips her to address a broad spectrum of psychological concerns

Caring for the community with commitment, compassion, and innovation, we aim to empower healthier lives through quality medical services and support at every stage.
